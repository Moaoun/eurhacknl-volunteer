About EurHackNL

The Netherlands' biggest student-built AI event

EurHackNL brings together the brightest student minds from across Europe to build, experiment, and compete at the frontier of AI. Organised entirely by students, for students.

Two days. 500+ builders. Real mentorship from 15+ sponsors. And a volunteer team that makes it all happen. That is where you come in.

Your Role

What you'll be doing

🤝

Guest Management

Welcome guests and speakers, keep arrivals running smoothly, and make sure everyone feels at home from the moment they walk in.

🗺️

Participant Coordination + Navigation

Help participants find their way around campus and connect them to the right places throughout both days.

🎟️

Ticket Check-In

Verify participant entry at the door and keep the flow moving efficiently from the start.

💬

Information and Routing

Be the go-to point for questions on the day. Connect participants to the right people fast.

After you apply, we will schedule a short meetup with all volunteers before the event to go through every position in detail, answer questions, and make sure everyone feels fully prepared.
