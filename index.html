About EurHackNL

The Netherlands' biggest student-built AI event

EurHackNL brings together the brightest student minds from across Europe to build, experiment, and compete at the frontier of AI. Organised entirely by students, for students — two days of intense building, real mentorship, and connections that last.

Every part of the event depends on a dedicated volunteer team. If you want a behind-the-scenes view of what it takes to run a 500-person hackathon — this is it.
